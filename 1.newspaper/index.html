Pimento Cheese And Heirloom Tomato Tart With Ritz Cracker Crust

This could quite possibly be the best thing you make this summer.

By Kelly Paige

What better way to celebrate tomato season than with a recipe highlighting summer’s finest offering? Heirloom tomatoes are gorgeous, juicy, come in a rainbow of colors and are packed full of delicious flavor.

Pimento Cheese and Heirloom Tomato Tart with Ritz Cracker Crust

Makes 1 (9-inch) tart

Ingredients

  • 2 sleeves Ritz crackers (about 7.5 ounces or 1 3/4 cup crushed crackers)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 10 tablespoons butter, melted

Directions

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  • Add crackers to bowl of food processor. Process until crackers are a fine crumb. Add Parmesan cheese, melted butter and 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt. Process until evenly combined and mixture holds together when pressed between fingers. If it doesn’t hold together, add an additional tablespoon of melted butter.
